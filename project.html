Dr. Altayeb Salih

Sudanese Writer And Journalist

About

Writer & Journalist

He was born on 12 July 1929 Alduba, Sudan

Al-Tayeb Salih...His full name is Al-Tayeb Muhammad Salih Ahmad, He is a Sudanese writer and journalist, considered one of the most famous Arab writers in the twentieth century, He is famous for his novel and short stories; Critics called him "the genius of the Arabic novel." Al-Tayeb Salih worked as a journalist for the British Broadcasting Corporation (BBC) and for several Arab magazines, His novels and short stories have been translated into more than ten languages.
